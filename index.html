plan.research-data.no

is a knowledge resource to support Data Management planning at Norwegian research institutions.

For researchers writing a DMP

DMP questionnaire

Start writing a DMP using the tool Data Stewardship Wizard/ FAIR Wizard. You can select between different DMP questionnaires to adapt for project-specific needs.

Accompanying information resources

Information pages for researchers filling out a DMP, supplementing the DMP questionnaire in Data Stewardship Wizard/ FAIR Wizard.

Find local and discipline-specific experts

Find the local DMP experts to support you and get inspired by discipline-rooted resources.

About data management planning

Purpose of a Data Management Plan

Why should you write a Data Management Plan (DMP)?

Guiding questions pre-start/ pre-award

Identify data management needs and challenges in ahead of starting on a project, particularly beneficial when applying for funding.

Glossary

Terms used in the context of data management plans and research data management explained.

For supporting staff

DMP Background knowledge

The knowledge base for support personnel includes the annotation of the Science Europe DMP guidance in the Norwegian context.

Conceptual DMP reflections

The project work involved conceptual reflections on DMPs which we believe can be of value to other national and international DMP projects.

We welcome your contributions!

plan.research-data.no is an open resource created by the University libraries at UiB, NTNU, UiO, UiT, ELIXIR Norway and PhD-on-Track. Everyone is welcome to contribute to this community project or suggest improvements! To cite plan.research-data.no, please use the following format:

plan.research-data.no. DMP Support Package for Norwegian Higher Education Libraries. URL: https://plan.research-data.no
